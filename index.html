About Us

Built on
Expertise &
Integrity

Nyawo Group is a proudly South African multi-disciplinary company operating at the intersection of construction and telecommunications. With roots going back to 2015, we have grown from a single civil works contractor into a full-service infrastructure group.

Our teams have delivered roads, pipelines, fibre networks, and wireless deployments across every corner of South Africa — driven by a commitment to quality, community, and long-term partnerships with both public and private sector clients.

As a BBB-EE compliant business, we are invested not just in building infrastructure, but in building the future of South Africa.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every project, every team member, and every decision at Nyawo Group.

01

Quality Without Compromise

Every structure we build and every network we deploy is held to the highest standard — because South Africa deserves infrastructure built to last.

02

Community First

We are a South African company, serving South African communities. Our growth is tied to the upliftment of the people and regions we operate in.

03

Reliable Delivery

Deadlines matter. Budgets matter. Our clients count on us to deliver on time and in scope — and we build our reputation project by project.

04

Safety Always

From civil worksites to telecoms towers, the safety of our teams and surrounding communities is non-negotiable in everything we do.

05

Partnership Over Transaction

We invest in long-term relationships with clients, suppliers, and communities — not just contracts. Trust is our most valuable asset.

06

Continuous Growth

Since 2015, we have never stopped learning. Our teams stay at the frontier of construction techniques and telecommunications technology.
